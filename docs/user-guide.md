## The Users
There are two main users of our system. 
- Primary user: Those who only want to obtain aggregrated flight results of their desired travel plans to easily compare flights based on airline, price, duration, and stops.
- Secondary user: Those who want to travel but need/want help deciding on the destination. These users can access our destination suggestion feature by creating an account, signing in, and then completing the Search Preference form to indicate their travel preferences ranging from travel activity to climate. 

## Main Tasks
The following are main tasks our two main users can perform.
- Both users:
  - Fill out the Find Flight search form by inputting the departure location, destination location, travel dates, and number of travelers
  - Click the Search button to view aggregrated flight results of their desired travel plans
  - Create an account
  - Sign into their existing account
- Secondary users:
  - Click the Start Here button on the homepage to access and fill out the Search Preferences form
  - Click on the Find My Destination button on the Search Preferences form to access their destination suggestions
  - Complete the flight search form on the destination suggestions page
  - Select their destination suggestions from a drop-down menu in the Destination field of the destination suggestions page
  - Save flights into their signed-in account
  - View their saved flights in their signed-in account
  - Compare their saved flights

## Step-by-Step Workflow for Core Features
The following is a short step-by-step workflow for our core features:

Simple flight search feature:
1) Access homepage
2) Fill out the Find Flight search form by inputting the departure location, destination location, travel dates, and number of travelers
3) Click the Search button to navigate to a new page and view the aggregrated flight results

Discovering destination suggestion feature:
1) Access homepage
2) Click the Sign In button at the top right corner of the page
3a) Enter registered email and password to sign in OR
3b) Click the Create an Account button to create a new account
  3b1) Fill out the requested info on the Create Account page
  3b2) Click on the Create Account button to complete the process and create an account in the system
  3b3) Complete step 3a above
4) Click on the Start Here button on the homepage
5) Fill out the Search Preferences form that populates on a new page
6) Click the Find My Destination button at the bottom of the Search Preferences form
7) View the destination suggestions on a new page

Find, view, and save flight search based on destination suggestion feature:
1) Follow steps 1 through 7 in the "Discovering destination suggestion feature" section above
2) Enter the Departure location, Departure Date, Return Date, and Travelers fields in the flight search form at the top of the destination suggestions page
3) Select the desired destination suggestion from the Destination drop-down field of the flight search form
4) Click the Search button
5) Click the Save Flight button to save desired flight results

View saved flights feature:
1) Follow steps 1 through 3b in the "Discovering destination suggestion feature" section above
2) Click on the drop-down arrow or menu at the top right corner of any page that is next to the username (or email address before the '@' symbol)
3) Click on the View Saved Flights option in the drop-down menu to access a pop-up with saved flight results

Compare saved flights feature (for at least two saved flights):
1) Follow steps 1 through 3 in the "View saved flights feature" section above to save at least two flight results
2) Click on the Add to Compare button for at least two saved flights
3) Click on the Compare Flights button at the top right corner of the Saved Flights pop-up window
4) View the Compare Flights pop-up window to compare airlines, price, duration, and stops side-by-side

## Known Limitations or Expected Constraints
The following are our system's known limitations or expected constraints:
- Flight results only return airport codes and not city names
- There is a lag when users click the Search button in the flight search forms the first time where no results populate
- The View Details button of the flight results are not active
